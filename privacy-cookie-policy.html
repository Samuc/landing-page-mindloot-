Privacy Policy & Cookies Policy

This website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve user experience and analyze website usage.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

More information about cookies can be found at www.aboutcookies.org.

Any personal data provided will be used solely for legitimate business purposes such as responding to inquiries or providing information about our services.

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at contact@mindloot.tech.

This data is used exclusively to improve website quality and performance. You can opt out by disabling cookies in your browser.
